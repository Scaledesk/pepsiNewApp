Working Capital Completed 

Working capital management is the management of the short-term investment and financing of a company. The goal of Working Capital Management is to adequate cash flow for operations and most productive use of resources.

Treasury 101Completed 

As Sales is the face of the company for Customers, R & D for innovation, Reporting for Sector, Treasury is the face of the company for Banks. Treasury is the part of all activities where Banks act as key Stakeholders. Treasury in a company is key in determining the firm’s financial strategy and financial policy – advising on what businesses to invest in, organizing the appropriate funding for this, and controlling the risk in the organization.
